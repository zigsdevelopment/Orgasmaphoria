Upload checklist

Before publishing.

  • Confirm the creator has permission to publish the file.
  • Remove private drafts and personal information.
  • Use a safe file name and clear title.
  • Choose the least-public access level required.
  • Test the download with a matching member account.
  • Archive outdated versions instead of overwriting records silently.
