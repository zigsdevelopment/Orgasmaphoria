Privacy

Privacy notice starter.

This is a starter document for testing and must be reviewed for the final business, technology, jurisdiction, and data practices before launch.

Information a production service may collect

Account details, profile information, membership status, purchases, event RSVPs, saved resources, messages, contact submissions, privacy settings, accessibility preferences, device and security logs, and records needed for support or moderation.

How information may be used

To provide accounts and membership access, deliver purchased files, process event participation, support communication, protect the community, respond to inquiries, maintain security, comply with law, and improve the service where consent or another legal basis applies.

Member visibility

Profile fields and messages should follow the choices selected in Settings. Members should avoid posting private contact information or details they do not want shared with other members.

Payments

A secure hosted payment provider should process card information. Orgasmaphoria should receive transaction status and limited billing records rather than raw card numbers.

Retention and deletion

The final policy must state how long account, purchase, message, moderation, contact, security, and consent records are kept, plus how a verified user can request access, correction, export, or deletion where applicable.

This prototype

The downloadable prototype stores demonstration sessions, accounts, settings, messages, reports, uploads, cart items, and contact submissions in the current browser using localStorage and IndexedDB. Clearing site data removes most or all of it. No included form sends data to Orgasmaphoria.

Questions
