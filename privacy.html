Privacy

Privacy notice.

How Orgasmaphoria handles public-site visits, personal accounts, memberships, purchases, files, profiles, messages, support requests, and display preferences.

Information you provide

Depending on the services you use, the site may receive account identifiers, email address, display name, profile information, privacy choices, membership and purchase records, event responses, messages, support requests, and files submitted through authorized staff tools.

Payments

Payment information is entered on a hosted payment page operated by the payment provider. Orgasmaphoria receives transaction identifiers, purchase details, status, and limited billing information needed to provide access and support. The website does not store full payment card numbers.

Profiles and messages

Members control whether their profile appears in the directory and whether new direct messages are allowed. Messages are visible to conversation participants. Authorized moderation access should be limited to published safety, legal, and support purposes.

Private files and permissions

Member and staff files are stored outside the public website and delivered only after account permissions are checked. Access may come from a membership, a separate purchase, a staff role, or a manual permission grant.

Third parties

The site may use hosting, authentication, database, file storage, email, payment, spam prevention, and Spotify services. Those providers process information under their own terms and privacy practices.

Your choices

Account settings provide profile visibility, messaging, online-status, and display controls. Contact Orgasmaphoria for account access, correction, export, deletion, or privacy questions.

Contact

Use the contact form and choose “Privacy question.”
