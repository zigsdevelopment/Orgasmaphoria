# Content Administration Guide

## Access labels

- **Public:** no account required.
- **All signed-in members:** any approved account.
- **Inner Circle:** core paid membership and higher.
- **Velvet Patron:** highest member tier and staff.
- **Staff only:** internal operational material.

## Upload workflow

1. Confirm ownership, license, consent, and publication approval.
2. Remove private drafts, personal information, hidden metadata, and comments.
3. Give the resource a clear title, subtitle, description, type, and tags.
4. Choose the least-public access level that meets the purpose.
5. Upload the file and confirm server-side validation and malware scanning.
6. Preview with an account at the intended access level.
7. Publish and record the review date.
8. Archive outdated versions while retaining an audit history.

## Recommended content categories

- Music and release extras
- Books and digital booklets
- Printable games
- Activities and host guides
- Event invitations
- Workbooks and journals
- Podcast transcripts and guest resources
- Staff operations
- Legal, consent, and licensing records (staff only)

## Files that should not be uploaded to public hosting

- Member lists or personal contact information
- Payment data
- Passwords, secret keys, or access tokens
- Unreleased contracts or legal records
- Private manuscripts without explicit authorization
- Moderation records
- Identity documents
- Health, financial, or highly sensitive personal information
