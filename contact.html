Before contacting

Common questions.

The Site Guide includes more detailed instructions for accounts, privacy, messages, the library, events, purchases, and staff uploads.

How do I access member-only content?

Sign in, confirm your membership tier, then open the Library or Events page. Locked items explain which access level is required.

Can I control whether members message me?

Yes. Open Settings, choose Privacy, and change the direct-message setting. You can also block or report an account from its profile.

Where are purchased files stored?

In production, purchases should appear in the account library after the payment provider confirms the order. The current storefront is a non-payment demonstration.

How do staff upload a new book, game, or invitation?

Staff sign in, open the Staff Portal, complete the content details, select an access level, choose a file, and publish. The included demo stores files only in the current browser.
