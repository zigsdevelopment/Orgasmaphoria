Accessibility

Accessibility statement.

Orgasmaphoria aims to make its public pages, accounts, store, resources, and community features usable across devices and access needs.

Display controls

The floating Aa button provides text-size choices, higher contrast, reduced motion, underlined links, a simpler reading font, increased spacing, and appearance options.

Design approach

The site uses keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus indicators, semantic headings and landmarks, labeled forms, responsive layouts, alternative text, reduced-motion support, and readable color contrast.

Digital documents

Published documents should include meaningful reading order, searchable text, tagged headings, descriptive links, image descriptions where needed, and accessible alternatives when a format creates a barrier.

Request support

Use the contact form and choose “Accessibility request.” Describe the page, resource, barrier, and format or accommodation that would help.
