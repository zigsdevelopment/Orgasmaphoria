Accessibility

Accessibility statement.

Last updated July 2026

Orgasmaphoria aims to make this website usable by a wide range of visitors, including people using keyboards, screen readers, zoom, high contrast, reduced motion, and alternative reading preferences.

Built-in accessibility

  • Keyboard-visible focus indicators and a skip-to-content link.
  • Responsive layouts designed to reflow across screen sizes and zoom levels.
  • Text size, contrast, motion, link, font, spacing, and appearance preferences through the floating “Aa” button.
  • Semantic headings, landmarks, buttons, links, and labeled form controls.
  • Clear notices before loading the third-party Spotify player.

Third-party content

The Spotify player is provided by Spotify and may behave according to Spotify’s own accessibility support. The site also provides a direct link to the Spotify artist page so visitors can use their preferred Spotify application or interface.

Request assistance

Use the contact form and choose “Accessibility request.” Include the page, the task you were trying to complete, your preferred response method, and any relevant assistive technology. Do not include medical details that are not needed to explain the request.

Ongoing improvement

Accessibility is treated as an ongoing responsibility. New pages, media, documents, forms, account features, and third-party integrations should be reviewed as they are introduced.
