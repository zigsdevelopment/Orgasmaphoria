Accessibility

Accessibility statement.

Orgasmaphoria aims to make the public website and member portal usable by a wide range of visitors, including people using keyboards, screen readers, zoom, high contrast, reduced motion, and alternative reading preferences.

Built-in controls

  • Keyboard-visible focus indicators and a skip link.
  • Responsive layouts designed for zoom and small screens.
  • Text size, contrast, motion, link, font, spacing, and appearance preferences.
  • Labels, instructions, status messages, and accessible dialogs for interactive forms.
  • Semantic headings, landmarks, buttons, links, and form controls.

Known demonstration limits

This package is a front-end prototype. It has not received a formal third-party accessibility audit. Spotify embeds and browser PDF viewers may have accessibility behavior controlled by their providers. Example PDFs are visually structured but should be remediated and tagged before production publication where required.

Requesting help

Use the contact form and select Accessibility request. Explain the page, task, assistive technology, and preferred response method. Do not include sensitive medical information unless it is necessary to explain the request.

Ongoing work

Before launch, the team should test keyboard operation, focus order, screen readers, 200% and 400% zoom, reflow, color contrast, form errors, time limits, captions, transcripts, document accessibility, and third-party integrations.
