About the project

Use this biography for listings, interviews, show notes, and media coverage. The final version can be adjusted once your parents provide names, location, and origin story.

Orgasmaphoria is an independent music and spoken-word project creating an immersive world where romance, mystery, confidence, and emotional storytelling meet. Its sound and visual identity draw from cinematic atmosphere, gothic elegance, and the emotions people often keep hidden.

The project approaches mature themes with intention rather than shock. Music, spoken stories, and future conversations are designed around creativity, trust, communication, and self-expression between adults. Across every release, Orgasmaphoria aims to create a recognizable emotional universe: luxurious, intimate, mysterious, and unapologetically human.
