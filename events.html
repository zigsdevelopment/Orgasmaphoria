Private invitations

Limited-capacity events with clear access, privacy, conduct, accessibility, and participation information.

What every invitation should include

Clarity is part of hospitality.

  • Who may attend and whether membership is required
  • Date, start and end times, location, and time zone
  • Accessibility and accommodation information
  • Privacy, recording, and conduct expectations
  • Ticketing, cancellation, and contact information
An invitation is the first act of hospitality.
